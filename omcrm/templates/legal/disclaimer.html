Risk Disclaimer

Important information about trading risks and financial market exposure

Important Risk Warning

Trading financial instruments involves significant risk of loss and may not be suitable for all investors. The high degree of leverage can work against you as well as for you. Before deciding to trade financial instruments, you should carefully consider your investment objectives, level of experience, and risk appetite.

General Trading Risks

Market Volatility

Financial markets are subject to high volatility and can move rapidly against your positions, potentially resulting in significant losses.

Leverage Risk

Leveraged products can amplify both gains and losses. A small market movement can have a proportionally larger impact on your invested funds.

Liquidity Risk

Market conditions may prevent you from closing positions at favorable prices, especially during volatile market periods.

Market Hours Risk

Price gaps can occur when markets reopen after periods of closure, potentially causing significant losses.

Specific Instrument Risks

Cryptocurrency Trading Risks
  • Extreme Volatility: Cryptocurrencies can experience rapid and substantial price movements
  • Regulatory Risk: Changing regulations may affect cryptocurrency values and availability
  • Technology Risk: Technical issues, security breaches, or network failures may impact trading
  • Market Manipulation: Cryptocurrency markets may be subject to manipulation due to lower liquidity
Foreign Exchange (Forex) Risks
  • Currency Risk: Exchange rate fluctuations can result in significant gains or losses
  • Political Risk: Political events and economic policies can greatly impact currency values
  • Interest Rate Risk: Changes in interest rates can affect currency pair values
  • 24/7 Market: Continuous trading means positions are exposed to risk at all times
CFDs and Derivative Instruments
  • Unlimited Loss Potential: Losses can exceed your initial investment
  • Margin Requirements: Additional funds may be required to maintain positions
  • No Ownership Rights: CFDs do not confer ownership of underlying assets
  • Counterparty Risk: Exposure to the financial stability of the provider

Technical & Operational Risks

System Failures

Technical failures, including internet connectivity issues, platform malfunctions, or system downtime may prevent you from accessing your account or executing trades.

Order Execution

Orders may not be executed at the requested price due to market conditions, slippage, or technical issues.

Data Accuracy

Price feeds and market data may be delayed, inaccurate, or incomplete, which could affect trading decisions.

Security Risks

Cybersecurity threats, including hacking attempts and data breaches, may affect platform security and user accounts.

Past Performance Disclaimer

Past performance is not indicative of future results. Any trading history presented is for informational purposes only and should not be relied upon as a guarantee of future performance. Market conditions can change rapidly, and historical performance may not be repeated.
